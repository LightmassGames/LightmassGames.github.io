About Lightmass Games

Lightmass Games aims to create emotional experiences - both in games and in virtual media.

Founded by a former Shawnee State University gaming student, the studio started out as a means to an end for their capstone project Beyond Critical. Lightmass Games has since become a creative outlet and a reenvisioned dream for its founder Dalton Fox. The studio's future lies in creating community tools, multiplayer games, and emotional experiences.

Have an idea to share? Feel free to reach out to us via email, social media, or our Discord.
